About the Application

  • Soluship.com is a comprehensive shipping and logistics application designed specifically for the Canadian market.
  • The platform facilitates the shipment of envelopes, packages, and freight, providing users with an end-to-end solution for their shipping needs while ensuring compliance with Canadian shipping regulations.
  • The system integrates with multiple carriers, enabling users to compare competitive shipping rates across carriers in real time.
This project involved a comprehensive redesign of the SoluShip platform, transforming it into a modern, scalable solution that combines intuitive UX , seamless carrier integration, and robust performance to support both businesses and individuals in their daily shipping needs.

UX Audit

The original Soluship.com platform was functional but lacked user-centered design principles. The major usability issues included:

Complex navigation: Menu and submenu structure were inconsistent, making it difficult for users to locate features.
Slow rendering: Pages, especially shipment tracking and search, took considerable time to load, impacting efficiency.
Steep learning curve: Creating shipments was not intuitive‚Äîonly trained users could use it effectively.
Lack of modern UI standards: The interface didn‚Äôt align with best practices in shipping and logistics platforms.

Goal & Choosing a Design Direction

R&D across multiple competitors in the shipping and logistics industry (FedEx, DHL, UPS, etc.) to identify best practices and modern patterns.
Enhance user experience by applying user-centered design principles.
Improve efficiency and performance with a faster, more responsive interface.
Vertical navigation menus improved discoverability.
Clear distinctions between packages, freight, and rate calculation were standard across competitors.
Introduce a seamless tracking system accessible even without login, reducing friction for new and occasional users.

High-Fidelity Mockups

I contributed by designing high-fidelity mockups in Photoshop, focusing on the most critical flows: Shipment creation and Rate calculator. These mockups applied user-centered design principles, improving clarity and reducing friction in complex workflows.

A modern, professional look aligned with logistics industry standards.
Clear, step-by-step flow for shipment creation.
A simplified, readable rates page with immediate cost breakdown.

Conclusion

The redesign successfully modernized the shipping platform by improving usability, streamlining shipment creation, and enhancing efficiency. The outcome was a scalable, user-centered platform that set a new benchmark in the logistics domain.

About the Application

  • Snap Solutions is a healthcare platform designed to automate Employer Service Departments for clinics, enabling them to reduce costs, improve operational efficiency, and scale patient volumes.
  • The system focuses on delivering professional, automated clearance reports to employers‚Äîeliminating manual processes and improving turnaround times.
This project was initiated to redesign and redevelop an existing healthcare and employer service platform originally built on Caspio . While Caspio provided the basic workflows, the client faced limitations in terms of scalability, performance, and user experience.

UX Audit

During the initial audit, we identified critical usability and workflow gaps across three stakeholders ‚Äî Employers, Clinics, and Labs . The existing process was heavily manual, fragmented, and prone to delays in documentation and compliance reporting. The original system, developed in Caspio, provided basic workflows but had significant limitations in scalability, usability, and visual consistency . This audit gave us clear direction on where the existing platform was falling short and how a complete redesign could solve these issues. During the audit, we identified pain points such as:

Complex workflows with too many manual steps for clinics and employers.
Outdated user interface that did not align with modern usability standards.
Limited automation, requiring staff to handle repetitive tasks like follow-ups and report delivery.
Lack of a responsive design, creating barriers for mobile and tablet users.

Goals & Choosing a Design Direction

The redesign focused on building a modern, user-centered platform from scratch, addressing both functional and aesthetic gaps. The goals included:

Enhance usability: Simplify appointment booking, documentation, and reporting for clinics, employers, and labs.
Increase automation: Auto-notifications for physicals, drug screens, and lab results to reduce manual workload.
Modernize UI: Create a responsive, scalable, and professional interface optimized for accessibility.
Benchmark competitors: We researched modern SaaS platforms in the healthcare and compliance space to define best practices in navigation, dashboards, and reporting workflows.

We benchmarked leading SaaS healthcare platforms and defined a design direction centered on usability, accessibility, and collaboration with developers, ensuring the solution was both intuitive and technically achievable.

Figma Works

Wireframes: Rapid iterations tested with stakeholders to validate navigation and flows.
High-fidelity mockups: Designed in Figma, with a scalable component library for faster development and consistent UI.
User-centered design principles: Typography, spacing, and hierarchy optimized for readability and task efficiency.
Responsive layouts: Adapted for desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Conclusion

Fully modernized SaaS platform with a focus on scalability, automation, and user-centered design. By collaborating closely with development and stakeholders, we improved usability through streamlined workflows, automated notifications, and dynamic portals for clinics, employers, and labs.
